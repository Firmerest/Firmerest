Project Moon Videogames

  • Lobotomy Corporation
  • Library of Ruina
  • Limbus Company

Lobotomy Corporation

Lobotomy Corporation is the first game in the current Project Moon Trilogy, and it is a game heavily inspired by the works of the online SCP Foundation.

Revolves around commanding employees to interact with various "abnormalities" which employees work to extract resources from and contain.

Library of Ruina

Library of Ruina is a sequel to Lobotomy Corporation that follows the character Roland and the various librarians of The Library who search for the perfect book.

You invite the various cruel and unusual residents of The City to The Library and defeating them in gorgeous turn-based deck-building combat.

Limbus Company

Limbus Company is the final game in the trilogy which follows twelve "Sinners" based off classic literature as they search for "Golden Boughs" in The City

Combat revolves around a turn-based format akin to a simplified version of Ruina. You can obtain variants of characters ("Identifies) through a "Gacha" mechanic.
